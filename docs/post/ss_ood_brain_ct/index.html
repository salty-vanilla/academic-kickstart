Anomaly score

$$ score = (1-\lambda)s_g + \lambda s_r $$ $$ s_g = - \sum^N_i q_i \log g (\tilde{x_{q_i}}) $$ $$ s_r = \alpha \times | x_i-f(x_i) |_2 $$
